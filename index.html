About Me

I am a passionate and self-motivated learner with a strong interest in artificial intelligence, healthcare, and problem-solving. I enjoy exploring how technology can be applied to solve meaningful real-world challenges, particularly in the medical field.

My interest in AI has led me to build personal projects, learn programming through Codecademy, and experiment with AI tools to better understand how intelligent systems can assist people. I believe AI is not just about writing code—it is about creating solutions that improve lives.

Alongside technology, I have a deep interest in pharmaceuticals and medical science. My long-term vision is to combine these two fields by developing advanced AI systems that support healthcare professionals. Rather than replacing doctors, I hope to build AI that helps them make faster, more informed decisions.

Beyond academics, I enjoy continuously learning new skills. Whether it is reading, developing projects, improving my technical abilities, or challenging myself through independent learning, I strive to become a better version of myself every day.
